Nice To Meet You!

I'm a web designer with a passion for creating impactful and user-centric web experiences. My journey into the world of web design began during the 2020 shutdowns, igniting a deep passion for web development that has driven me to continuously enhance my skills.

With a solid foundation in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, along with experience in front-end frameworks like React, I specialize in developing responsive and visually appealing websites. Whether it's building a dynamic e-commerce site, designing a sleek portfolio for a creative professional, or optimizing web applications for performance, I thrive on bringing innovative ideas to life.

Before diving into web design, I spent 8 years managing concerts and live events, where I honed my skills in organization, attention to detail, and client communication. This background enriches my approach to web design, blending creativity with a strong understanding of user needs and project management.

I am excited to leverage my skills and experience to create engaging digital solutions that resonate with users and achieve your goals. Let's collaborate to bring your vision to life!
